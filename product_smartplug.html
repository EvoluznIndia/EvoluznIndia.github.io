Intellizens Smart Plug — Behind the Board

Embedded industrial single-phase power control with remote analytics for panel-level load management. operations

USE CASE

Enables panel-embedded control of single-phase devices with real-time analytics and remote monitoring via a centralized dashboard. Supports automated load scheduling and live energy visibility for air conditioning units, manufacturing auxiliaries, and other high-duty single-phase equipment. Ensures safer operations, reduces energy waste, and provides actionable insights for demand management and maintenance planning.

KEY FEATURES

Automated scheduling to run loads only when needed, extending equipment life and cutting energy waste.

Over-temperature protection auto-isolates circuits to prevent overheating and fire risks.

Remote control and monitoring through centralized dashboard or app with instant ON/OFF and status checks.

Power analytics and reporting identify energy-intensive devices and optimize runtime for cost savings.

Panel-mount “behind-the-board” design for industrial control cabinets and OEM integration.
