Intellizens Lighting Management System (ILMS)

Centralized intelligent lighting control for industrial, commercial, and office spaces.

USE CASE

Enables panel-level, centralized management of single- and multi-zone lighting with live telemetry and remote control. Upgrades existing lighting systems through automated scheduling, lux-based dimming, motion-triggered adjustments, and daylight harvesting to deliver consistent illumination while minimizing energy waste. Seamlessly integrates with Building Management Systems (BMS) and broader IIoT platforms for unified control, predictive maintenance, and rapid fault response based on occupancy and environmental changes.

BENEFITS
     
  • Precise, per-zone lighting control and energy analytics reduce operating costs and improve facility reliability.
    •  
  • Automated scheduling and adaptive dimming optimize energy use while maintaining compliant, high-quality lighting conditions.
    •  
  • Proactive alerts and predictive maintenance minimize downtime and service requirements.
    •  
  • Scalable for multi-zone deployments with seamless integration across industrial and commercial environments.
