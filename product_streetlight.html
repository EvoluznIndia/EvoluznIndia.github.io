Intellizens Smart Street Light (ISSL)

Intelligent, energy-efficient illumination for urban and industrial outdoor environments.

USE CASE

Provides autonomous, sensor-driven street and outdoor facility lighting with automated scheduling and real-time ambient light adjustment. Supports centralized remote monitoring and control via dashboard for rapid response to outages, occupancy changes, and weather conditions. Integrated analytics track energy consumption, component health, and maintenance requirements to optimize lifecycle costs for large-scale deployments.

BENEFITS
     
  • Consistent, energy-efficient outdoor lighting with automated dimming and motion-based controls reduces energy use and maintenance expenses.
    •  
  • Remote management, predictive alerts, and per-location analytics enable proactive maintenance and enhanced safety.
    •  
  • Designed for rugged outdoor conditions, scalable from single corridors to city-wide networks
    •  
  • Seamlessly integrates with IIoT ecosystems for comprehensive smart city applications.
