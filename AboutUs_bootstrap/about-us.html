Evoluzn Inc. is a wireless broadband devices ecosystem design, development, and realization company, formed under the laws of Illinois, USA in 2012. The headquarters of Evoluzn Inc. is located in Schaumburg, Illinois, USA, and operates internationally via its In-Country Support Offices.

Evoluzn Inc. was formed by a highly experienced team of engineers with diverse fields of expertise in wireless and wired broadband technologies such as LTE (4G), 3G (HSUPA, HSPA+, etc), CDMA, GSM, and Cable access technologies. We are passionate about devices design and development and believe in evolving the existing concepts for broader market reach and being socially responsible.

In 2019, the company began venturing into the IoT and Renewable Energy industry through establishing its very own Research and Development in India focusing in Smart Home and Energy Measuring devices. The goal is to be able to offer solutions that can deliver a unique experience, value, and intelligence- a step forward from the basic Remote Switching capability that most Smart Devices offer nowadays.

Currently, Evoluzn is located in the USA (HQ), Thailand, India, and the Philippines.

Mission: To deliver innovative solutions for Social and Environmental Challenges.

Vision: To be a major contributor in addressing the world’s Social and Environmental challenges.
